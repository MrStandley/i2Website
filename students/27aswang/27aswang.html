Ashley Wang

Hi! My name is Ashley Wang and I’m in my sophomore year here at SHC. Since attending, I’ve joined many of the clubs SHC has to offer, including Japanese club, tea club, cheese club, and fishing club. I’m also a member of the SHC choir, and the yearbook staff. I hope to learn and grow in all of these communities, and further my interests as time continues. Outside of school I love cooking, reading, and playing mahjong with my family. My love for cooking was born during the quarantine. I wanted to challenge myself in a skill other than academics, and I didn’t want to live off of microwave dinners once I lived on my own. I’m not a very good player when it comes to mahjong, but it’s become one of my favorite games. My family and I love to sit around a table and talk while we shuffle and match tiles. Through both mahjong and cooking, I’ve improved my relationships, management skills, and attentiveness.
